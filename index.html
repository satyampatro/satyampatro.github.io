PC.Tech

A web app built to quickly find software Engineers, Web Desiners, PC Technician. Users enter the search term and desired Issue, and are offered job listings from 6 of the most popular job sites in Ireland

Technology

PC.Tech uses Bootstrap3 for a mobile-first layout, with jQuery for easy page manipulation. On the backend an Express/Node.js app lives on a Hasura dyno that acts as a middleware to scrape job sites and return the information to the user.
